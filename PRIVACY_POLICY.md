# Privacy Policy for The Baker

**Effective Date:** 2026-01-27

## 1. Introduction
"The Baker" ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information Collection
We do not collect any personal data or personally identifiable information (PII) from our users.

### 2.1. Local Data
All data created within the app (recipes, timers, settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.

### 2.2. Permissions
The app requests the following permissions to function:
- **Alarms & Reminders (`SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM`)**: Used solely for the timer functionality to alert you when baking steps are due.
- **Notifications (`POST_NOTIFICATIONS`)**: Used to display timer alerts.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We use Firebase (Google) for:
- **Crashlytics**: To collect anonymous crash reports to help us fix bugs.
- **Analytics**: To understand basic usage patterns (e.g., active user counts).

These services may collect anonymous identifiers (like Advertising ID) subject to their own privacy policies.
